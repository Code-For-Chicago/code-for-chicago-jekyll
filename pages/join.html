How do we start projects?

Code for Chicago is focused on making projects shoulder-to-shoulder with our partners.

Start by Listening

At the onset we'll gather info about the problem space with our parners.

Identifying Skills

In our initial scoping we'll identify the technical needs for the project.

Teamwork

Partners are expected to be collaborators throughout the process.
